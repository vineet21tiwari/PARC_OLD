Baba Kalyani motivates the PARC team

February 21, 2020 Sunil Nanda

Padmabhushan, Shri Babasaheb Neelkanth Kalyani, Chairman Bharat Forge, is a BITS alumnus from the 1965-70 batch. He is the Founder Chairman of Pratham Pune Education Foundation, an NGO that is engaged in providing primary education to children belonging to under-privileged sections of the local community.

Shri Kalyani spent an hour at PARC on 21-Feb-2020, though he was scheduled for only 10min. He witnessed the PARC activities first-hand and interacted enthusiastically with the PARC team. He shared his experience as a social innovator and offered a lot of points to ponder over. One big take-away was that PARC should use more technology to help its cause. His visit was a morale booster and motivator for the entire team on the ground in Pilani.

We are thankful for such uplifting words of wisdom and encouragement from stalwarts.

Thank you, Sir!
