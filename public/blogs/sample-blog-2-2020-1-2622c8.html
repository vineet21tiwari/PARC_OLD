Stress Management Workshop

January 26, 2020 Sunil Nanda

In collaboration with MPOWER, PARC organized a one day workshop on Stress Management on 25-Jan-2020. The session was held at Balika Vidyapeeth, Pilani, with over 60 teachers in attendance. The teachers, in turn, will be spreading the awareness and techniques among their students.

The session was presided over by Prof. Sudhirkumar Barai , Director, BITS Pilani. Also attending were Dr. Chandra Shekhar, Professor Emeritus BITS Pilani, Dr. V. K. Dube, PARC Director and Dr. M. Kasturi, Principal, Birla Balika Vidyapeeth.

This is the first in the series of mental health workshops planned in conjunction with MPower (https://www.mpowerminds.com/aboutus)
