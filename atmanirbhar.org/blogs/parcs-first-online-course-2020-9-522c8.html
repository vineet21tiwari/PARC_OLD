PARC'S FIRST ONLINE COURSE

September 5, 2020 Team AtmaNirbhar

Digital literacy is more important than ever. In today’s day and age, the world seems to have shifted to the internet. Mainly primary education in rural India was content to let our excellent teachers work their magic in the classroom, limiting their impact to within four concrete walls. However, recent events have forced change upon us. The teachers no longer have the reach of the means to connect with their students. Students are deprived of a fundamental bond that shapes their perspective and future for no fault of their own. They’re deprived of their third home.

 At PARC, we believe that no student should be left behind and want everyone to have equal access to quality education and teaching.  We must help equip teachers with the necessary tools they need to survive and thrive in this new, online world that will help them teach their students better. To help you with this transition, we have curated a course that has video tutorials, PDF documents on a plethora of useful online tools, such as Search Engines, Google Services, and video conferencing solutions. The course also includes an assessment to see how far you have come for each lesson. After completing the course, you can certify yourself by taking the final assessment. The course has been completely designed and developed by students from BITS Pilani. 

The destiny of our future generation lies in the hands of the educator; it is up to them to use the best possible tools available to shape it. We provide those tools. You do the rest. So, join us on this beautiful and exciting journey as we, the Student Division of PARC, trace various software and technology that will help you change a student’s life.
